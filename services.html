Our Services

Discover the comprehensive range of services offered by SERVYX CERTIFY, tailored to meet the needs of exporters, importers, and manufacturers in the MENA region.

Product Certification

SERVYX CERTIFY provides consultation for quality certificates meeting requirements for UAE/KSA/International standards and guarantees the highest standards, meeting our customers' expectations for prompt delivery and consumer safety.

KSA Certification

We assist with obtaining SASO Quality Mark and SABER Certificate. The Saudi government has set regulatory standards to ensure that products meet the standard mark for safety and quality.

  • SASO
  • SABER
  • E-COSMA & SFDA

UAE Certification

We help with product registration in Dubai Municipality, ensuring that products meet the safety guidelines set by the municipality.

  • MOIAT (ESMA): ECAS, EQM
  • Municipality: MONTAJI, FIRS
  • TRDA (Telecommunication Devices)

Laboratory Testing

Our laboratory testing services are performed according to ISO 17025 standards for various products, ensuring compliance and safety.

  • Cosmetics
  • Perfumes
  • Textiles
  • Food Contact Materials
  • Bricks, Tiles, Ceramics, and Sanitary Ware
  • Footwear and Leather Products
  • Paper Products
  • Packaging
  • RoHS

Management System Consultation

We provide consultation services to assist you in obtaining the certification you seek. Our technical expertise ensures compliance with international certification requirements.

  • ISO 22000 Consultation, Implementation, and Certification
  • HACCP
  • ISO 9001 Consultation, Implementation, and Certification
  • GMP Consultation, Implementation, and Certification

Training & Development

We provide certified training courses through accredited bodies, ensuring that your team is knowledgeable and compliant with relevant standards.

  • ISO 9001 Lead Auditor Awareness Training
  • ISO 22000 Lead Auditor Awareness Training
  • ISO 45001 Lead Auditor Awareness Training
  • HALAL Awareness Training
