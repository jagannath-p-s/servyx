Why Product Certification is Important?

A product's certification indicates that it is safe and trustworthy. It serves as a barometer of product quality and a navigation for finding reputable manufacturers. Risk can be reduced and minimized by reputable companies. Their commitment to quality and safety is demonstrated by product certification.

Product certification is frequently required by government purchasing rules. Companies, colleges, and other organizations are following suit. The availability of low-cost, quick-to-market items is growing, making product certification more crucial than ever.

UAE Services

Product Registration in Dubai Municipality

The main purpose of Product Registration is consumer safety. Dubai Municipality holds adequate information to evaluate the safety of products. If the product meets the guidelines set by Dubai Municipality, you will receive a product registration certificate once it is approved or registered. Note that every product must be registered in Dubai Municipality before being placed on the market, even if the products are imported for re-export purposes.

Categories of Product Registration:

  • Cosmetic Product Registration
  • Detergent and Disinfectant Product Registration
  • Food Product Registration
  • Health Supplement Products Registration

Why is Product Registration mandatory in UAE?

Products Registration is necessary as each product imported into Dubai for local trading must be registered with Dubai Municipality and receive a registration certificate, which is required at the time of shipment clearing. The process ensures each cosmetic & packaged food product has all necessary information on its label, helping consumers understand what they are buying. Dubai Municipality checks products for any harmful substances or restricted components, rejecting non-compliant items.

Registration Regulation in UAE

The Dubai Government standardizes the manufacture, sale, and importation of cosmetic products by requiring that all cosmetic products be registered before being placed on the market. Additionally, individuals must obtain a proper license in Dubai related to cosmetics products.

Cosmetic Products Registration in Dubai

Cosmetic products are any substances or products intended to be placed in contact with external parts of the human body (e.g., epidermis, hair system, nails, lips) or with the teeth and mucous membranes of the oral cavity, for purposes such as cleaning, perfuming, changing appearance, correcting body odors, protecting, or keeping these parts in good condition.

Why is it Mandatory?

Before placing cosmetic products in the UAE local market, each product must be registered with Dubai Municipality. It is mandatory to obtain a certificate for cosmetic product label assessment from Dubai Municipality, ensuring consumer safety and helping understand the product's purpose.

Food Products Registration

Dubai serves as a major hub for imports and re-exports to Asia & North African countries. Food Products are among the main sectors imported into Dubai and re-exported to other regions. Therefore, Food Product Registration in Dubai is compulsory to ensure the safety and quality of imported and re-exported food, while facilitating food trade through simple and prompt procedures.

Health Supplement Products Registration

The purpose of Health Supplement Product Registration is to ensure compliance with endorsed conditions and specifications, preventing the rejection of shipments at Dubai's port or expropriation from the local market. Health Supplement Product Registration is mandatory for importing or placing these products in the local market.

ESMA/MOIAT

ESMA/MOIAT

ESMA/MOIAT has implemented two main schemes to ensure local and imported products comply with UAE National Standards: the Emirates Conformity Assessment Scheme (ECAS) and the Emirates Quality Mark (EQM).

Emirates Conformity Assessment Scheme (ECAS)

ECAS is a mandatory program introduced in 2004 to examine compliance with UAE standards for goods, both imported and domestic, to be sold in the country.

Emirates Quality Mark (EQM)

The EQM is a mark of conformity granted to products that comply with UAE National Standards and are manufactured by organizations with an effective Quality Management System. EQM certification is essential for shipment clearance and is mandatory for specific products like bottled drinking water and most juices.

Types of Products Regulated by ESMA/MOIAT:

  • Honey Products
  • Milk & Dairy Products
