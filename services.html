Why Product Certification is Important?

A product's certification indicates that it is safe and trustworthy. It serves as a barometer of product quality and a navigation for finding reputable manufacturers. Risk can be reduced and minimized by reputable companies. Their commitment to quality and safety is demonstrated by product certification.

Product certification is frequently required by government purchasing rules. Companies, colleges, and other organizations are following suit. The availability of low-cost, quick-to-market items is growing, making product certification more crucial than ever.

UAE Services

Product Registration in Dubai Municipality

The main purpose of product registration is consumer safety. If the product meets the guidelines set by Dubai Municipality, you will receive a product registration certificate. Every product must be registered before placing it on the market, even if the products are imported for re-export purposes.

  • Cosmetic Product Registration
  • Detergent and Disinfectant Product Registration
  • Food Product Registration
  • Health Supplement Products Registration

ESMA/MOIAT

ESMA/MOIAT implements two main schemes: the Emirates Conformity Assessment Scheme (ECAS) and the Emirates Quality Mark (EQM). These schemes ensure that local and imported products comply with UAE National Standards.

  • Cosmetics and Perfumes
  • Honey Products
  • Milk & Dairy Products
  • Low Voltage Equipment
  • Juices and Beverages
  • Drinking Water
  • Electronic Cigarettes

Telecommunications and Digital Regulatory Authority (TDRA)

We assist in ensuring compliance with TDRA regulations for telecommunication devices. Proper certification is crucial for importing and selling these devices within the UAE.
